I did this course kind of like a start to my journey into the machine learning world. I thought it would be better to 
revisit my fundamentals that I learned in my bachelors and also learn a bit more about linear algebra while at it.

This course was amazing! I am glad that I picked this course as Mr. Strang teaches linear algebra like a poet. Thanks to him it was easy to 
follow along the course and the exercises were really good as well. 

Since this was my first time doing an academic course after working as a professional for 4 years, 
I was a bit slow and it took a bit of time to get used to learning again. I am glad I picked the right course to get back into learning!
